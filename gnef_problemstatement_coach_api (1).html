💡 Examples: maternal health, infection prevention, data quality, HIV/TB, medication management, community outreach…
Step 4 of 6
What gap or poor outcome have you observed — and for how long?
⚠️ Describe the problem, not the solution. Avoid "We need more X" — instead describe what is happening or not happening. Include how long you've been noticing it.
Step 5 of 6
Do you have a number or metric — and what should it be compared against?
📐 A strong problem statement needs a specific figure (e.g. 62%, 3 out of 10) AND a benchmark to compare it to (e.g. national target of 95%, WHO standard, last year's rate). If you don't have the exact number yet, use a placeholder like [X%].
Step 6 of 6
Now write your problem statement
📐 The Problem Statement Formula
WHO / WHERE + WHAT (the gap) + NUMBER + TIME PERIOD + vs. BENCHMARK
Example At Greenfield Clinic, only 58% of ANC clients completed four or more antenatal visits between January and March 2026, compared to the national standard of 80%.
